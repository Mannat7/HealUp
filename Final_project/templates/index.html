  • About HealUp

    HealUp is a web-application aimed at making our heathcare system more efficient and comfortable for all. It's a platform currently able to recognize face and provide the details about the patient's last visit to the hospital. In future, we aim at making it able to consolidate and maintain the medical details of a patient. It can be used for a hostpital's website, so whenever a patient visits any branch of the hospital anywhere, his face will be recognised by the face recognition system and his details will easily be accessible to the hospital staff and they'll be able to start the treatment accordingly.

    The medical history of the patients plays a vital role in the further treatment and hence, transparency is important here. So, HealUp is here to do just that!
